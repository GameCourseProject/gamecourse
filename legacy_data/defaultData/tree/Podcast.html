Podcast

The idea is for you to create a podcast episode. A podcast, in case you aren't familiarized with the concept, is an audio show distributed online, usually on a regular basis. There are podcasts of the most varied types you can imagine, from a single guy ranting about a subject to panel discussions to authors reading books. They often include theme songs, separators and audio special effects. Think of them as modern day radio shows.

You can tackle this task in two different ways:

A short story podcast:
many authors read their stories online in podcast format, a chapter a week (ex: the former Podiobooks). Choose a short story of your liking and record an episode where you read and dramatize it. What's expected? A theme song, author intro before starting the story (telling us about what is going to be read), closing music, different voices for different characters and special effects where relevant. For instance, if you read about a gun being fired, it would be nice to hear the bang in the background (just make sure it doesn't interfere with the understanding of the narration).
Show on a subject of your interest
Like scifi movies? Then make a show about that. Comment on comic books. Discuss the latest android apps in the market. Etc. The format is similar to that for a short story (intro, effects, etc.) but you'll have to script it yourself instead of just reading someone's tale aloud.

We expect something of around 3-5 min in length. You should attach to your post the resulting mp3 file and the script (either written by you or the short story you read). In the post include a short description of what you did. And, you know it: present original work, and we may ask for improvements/changes before accepting the result, especially if the quality or originality requirements aren't met.
