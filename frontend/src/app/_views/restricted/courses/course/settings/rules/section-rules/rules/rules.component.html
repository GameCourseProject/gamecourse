What are rules?

Rule are a ser of statements that determine the behavior of the course. With them, we are able to define how to calculate the courses' awards and deliver them to students.


They are composed by a name, description, tags, and when and then clause.

When Clause

The when clause contains the requisites that need to be fulfilled in order to trigger the rule.


Usually, rules get logs in the when clause, which represent information with all the students' participations in the course.


For instance, to get all participations of students in lab nº2 we would write:

get_lab_logs(target, 2)

Then Clause

The then clause contains the effects of what will happen if the preconditions of the When clause are met.


Usually, rules define what will be rewarded to students in the then clause.


For instance, in order to award a grade of 18 points in exam 'Exam' to a student defined as target, we would write:

award_exam_grade(target, "Exam", logs, 18)

Use the extra help!

The Rule Editor counts with additional tools where supplementary help is displayed if needed.


This includes a reference manual, where all functions are defined and explained, as well as their parameters.
