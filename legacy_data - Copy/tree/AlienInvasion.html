Alien Invasion

For Alien Invasion the goal is for you to produce, using image edition, a "photo" of a building/city of your choice being invaded by aliens! Fire, explosions, ray guns, running people, let's have it all (or some :))! An example can be found below:

In essence, we expect a more careful/professional looking result than what you may already be accomplishing in the labs or other challenges. If you can make it especially aesthetically appealing and/or amusing all the better (alien invasion of IST, anyone?!)

You can do this using your own photos (probably much more interesting and fun?) or using photos downloaded from the Internet. There are plenty online tutorials on how to do this kind of thing (use different photos, obviously). The requirements, in short:

  • Original!
  • High quality (no visible discontinuities or differences in lighting, etc.)
  • Don't give us just the final result. In your post, please include the starting photo, end result and short description of the main steps you had to go through to get from A to B
  • As is true for all these awards, we the professors may ask for improvements/changes before accepting the result, especially if the quality or originality requirements aren't met.

Take care with avoiding the following if you want your contribution to be accepted and graded well.

  • Simply applying filters or using some "canned-effects" app and, in general, overdoing it...
  • Clearly pasted in ships/aliens/flames/etc. Not realistic.



  • Unrealistic settings. See below: wouldn't all those people be running away in panic?
  • Get the light right. See those (pasted in...) flames, below? Why aren't the surrounding buildings illuminated by them? Same goes for beams, energy weapons, ship drives and the like. Things cast light and produce shadows!
